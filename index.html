Rules

1. You will play with a 40 card deck constructed out of the two intro decks, three booster packs, and any number of basic lands you desire.

2. Decks may contain no more than three of the same card.

3. You will receive your pairings at lunch time and should play your match at 12 noon regardless of what lunch period you have.

4. The match will consist of best of three games following normal magic rules. The result (including game score) should be emailed to me at tsanmigu@andrew.cmu.edu by the following morning. Failure to do so will be considered a draw.

5. This week is a world cup style tournament. It will begin with a round robin group stage that is played on Tuesday-Thursday. The top three players in each group will advance to a single elimination bracket. Seeding will be determined by your current victory point total.

6. Victory Points will be awarded as follows: 1 VP will be awarded for each win in the group stage. 1 VP will be awarded for advancing out of the group stage. 2 VP will be awarded for winning your group. 3 VP will be awarded for the first win in the elimination stage, 3 VP for the second win, 4 VP for the third win, 6 VP for the fourth win, and 8 VP for the fifth win

7. Sideboarding is now permitted. You may make a 15 card sideboard, and my interchange the contents of your deck and sideboard in between games.

8. Trading is permitted with any other player playing magic this week.

9. Use of cards we did not give you will result in an immediate banning.
