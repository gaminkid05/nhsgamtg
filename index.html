Rules

Week 2 rules are as follows:

1. You will play with a thirty card deck

2. You will play best 2 out of 3 games against your opponent during lunch time

3. Failure to complete a match and report the results to tsanmigu@andrew.cmu.edu before the next day will be considered a loss for both players

4. Sideboarding is not permitted once a match begins

5. Each player's deck must be constructed from their two intro decks and the two booster packs they will receive at the start of week 2. You can request basic lands if you need them.

6. Trading is now permitted

7. Victory points will be awarded based on record. 4-0 earns 8 VP, 3-1 earns 4 VP, 2-2 earns 1 VP.
