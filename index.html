Rules

Week 2 rules are as follows:

1. You will play with a thirty card deck

2. You will play best 2 out of 3 games against your opponent during lunch time (or whenever is convenient). It is your responsibility to find your opponent and play against them.

3. Failure to complete a match and report the results to tsanmigu@andrew.cmu.edu before the next day will be considered a loss for both players

4. Sideboarding is not permitted once a match begins

5. Each player's deck must be constructed from their two intro decks and the two booster packs they will receive at the start of week 2. You can request basic lands if you need them. You may not include more than 4 copies of the same card in your deck

6. Trading is now permitted. You may not trade with students who are not participating in this tournament.

7. Victory points will be awarded based on record. 4-0 earns 8 VP, 3-1 earns 4 VP, 2-2 earns 2 VP, 1-3 earns 1 VP
