Rules

RULES FOR TOURNAMENT PLAYERS:

1. Matches will be played as a best of 5 games

2. Matches will be played with a 60 card deck built from all of the cards we have given you

3. Trading is permitted among players WHO ARE STILL ALIVE IN THE TOURNAMENT. (No trading with someone who was eliminated)

4. Play your matches at Noon regardless of what lunch you are in. They will be played on the RPIS tables.

5. I will personally supervise the matches and ensure all rules are followed. I will resolve any in-game rules conflicts

6. Sideboarding is permitted after game 2 of the match(from your entire pool of cards)

7. All results must be reported to me by end of day. Failure to complete your match will be a forfeiture of the tournament.

8. Victory Points will be awarded based on the number of wins you receive. 2 VP for the first win, 4 VP for the second win, 6 VP for the third win, and 12 VP for winning the tournament.

9. There will be a playoff for third and fourth. Third place will receive 4 extra VP.


RULES FOR ELIMINATED PLAYERS

1. Any player not in the tourney can get pairings on Tuesday - Friday this week.

2. Matches will be best two out of three

3. Games will be with 40 card decks.

4. Trading is allowed with all non-tournament-live players.

5. Sideboarding is permitted.

6. Play your matches whenever is convenient.

7. Report your matches by the following day.

8. Each win you receive will be worth 1VP. You will get 2 VP at the end of the week if you are undefeated this week in non-tournament matches and played at least 2 matches.
