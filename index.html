Rules

RULES FOR DRAFT PLAYERS:

1. Matches will be played as a best of 3 games

2. Matches will be played with a 40 card deck built from the cards that you drafted and any number of basic lands

3. Drafting will proceed as follows: each player will begin with 3 sealed booster packs. They will each open one booster pack, remove the basic land and token, and then select a card from the pack. They will then pass the remaining cards to the left. This repeats until the pack is exhausted. Repeath this for each of the three packs, except for in the second pack you pass cards to the right.

4. We will do the draft on Monday, then play the matches on Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday.

5. The draft shall be swiss- each player will play 3 matches.

6. The player who goes 3-0 shall be our champion, and will receive 50 Victory Points. 2-1 will earn 10 Victory Points. 1-2 will earn 2 Victory Points.

7. Trading between draft pools, or augmenting your pool with personal cards is strictly prohibited. Doing this will result in immediate forfeiture of the draft and all of your victory points.

8. Prizes will be distributed to all players based on the percentage of the total victory points each player (including non drafting players) possesses. If you got at least 5VP your are guarenteed a pack.

9. Players will keep any cards that they draft.


Thank you for participating in MTG this year!
