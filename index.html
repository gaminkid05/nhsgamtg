Rules

1. You will play with a 40 card deck constructed out of the two intro decks, three booster packs, and any number of basic lands you desire. 2. Decks may contain no more than three of the same card 3. You will receive your pairings at lunch time and should play your match at 12 noon regardless of what lunch period you have. 4. The match will consist of best of three matches. The result (including game score) should be emailed to me at tsanmigu@andrew.cmu.edu by the following morning. Failure to do so will be considered a draw. 5. This week is a world cup style tournament. It will begin with a round robin group stage that is played on Tuesday-Thursday. The top two players in each group will advance to a single elimination bracket. 6. Victory Points will be awarded as follows: 1 VP will be awarded to any player that wins a match in the group stage. 1 VP will be awarded for advancing out of the group stage. 2 VP will be awarded for the first win in the elimination stage, 3 VP for the second win, 3 VP for the third win, 4 VP for the fourth win, and 6 VP for the fifth win (for a total of 20 if you win) 7. Sideboarding is now permitted. You may make a 15 card sideboard, and my interchange the contents of your deck and sideboard in between games. 8. Trading is permitted with any other player playing magic this week.
